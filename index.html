This is my directing section. Here you can find all of the videos that I have directed. While each video has been directed by me, some have also been edited, written, and produced by myself also. Annie



This was my final project from college. I directed, wrote, edited and produced this film. It follows the story of Gordon. After the death of his mother, he finds himself having hallucinations of a girl named Annie.





Line of Duty Recreated



My university gave me the task of recreating a scene from British TV police drama Line of Duty. This was my first project since the Covid-19 pandemic began and despite the issues it caused I was happy with the result. This is also my first time working with a dolly and other specialist equipment. Original scene from series 4 episode 4.




Kit Kat Ad



This is the first advert I have made. When I was given the brief to make an advert I was also given a list of companies. I immediately decided I wanted to do Kit Kat as the idea came to me instantly. This was a great video to film and one of my favourite pieces that I’ve made.

Hello. My name is Neil Dudman Robb, I am a student filmmaker based in Edinburgh and founder of N.A.D.R Productions. For as long as I can remember I have had an intense passion for cinema and television. For years I have dreamed of being able to make films of my own and share my passion with the world. N.A.D.R is my first step into making this dream into a reality. I am about to graduate from Edinburgh Napier university with honours in television and plan on making more of my own movies through NADR Productions. Here on this site you can find some examples of my work as well as keep updated about upcoming projects of mine. I hope to add more regularly. So, welcome and enjoy your stay.
